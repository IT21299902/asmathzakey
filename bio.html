Hello, my name is Mohamed Asmath Zakey, and I am from the beautiful coastal town of Negombo in Sri Lanka. I pursued my secondary education at Dhammissara National College in Nattandiya and continued on to Maristella College in Negombo to take my advanced level exam. As the only child of my parents, I have always strived to make them proud and become successful in life.

From a young age, I developed a keen interest in the field of IT and its various applications. This passion led me to choose a career in cybersecurity, and I am currently pursuing my studies at SLIIT. With a focus on this dynamic and ever-evolving field, I am excited to explore new opportunities and contribute my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact..
